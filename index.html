ImageSpy

ImageSpy helps ensure that all uploaded images are being utilized within your HTML email campaigns.

How to Use:
  1. Drag and drop or upload your campaign images.
  2. Paste your HTML content into the provided textarea.
  3. Click the "Test" button to verify if all images are being used.
  4. Any unused images will be displayed below.

Images not being used:

    All images are being used.
